Veta · Brand mark · Board review

Three marks for the seam that becomes a forecast

Veta means an ore vein / seam — the hidden line you follow to the value. The product fuses fragmented signals into a forward demand forecast. The mark should say that in one stroke, read at favicon size, and survive in a single color. Each concept below appears as a lockup and at large + 16 px scale, on dark and on light.

01

Seam Forecast

the seam IS the forecast Chosen — new official mark

One continuous luminous line. It runs flat and dim on the left (history, buried in the rock), strikes a sharp inflection, and rises copper-to-amber on the right (demand projected forward). The bend reads as an implicit V-valley — the name — and the rising arm is literally the demand curve from the tagline, “See demand before it’s an order.” A teal signal node marks the inflection: the moment the seam stops describing the past and starts predicting. One idea, one gesture, nothing decorative. Favicon viability: a single bent line with a node — the strongest read at 16 px of the three, and the only one that still says “forecast,” not just “V,” when tiny.

02

Strata V

a seam splitting rock into a V Runner-up

A crisp geometric V formed by two layers of strata (offset rock bands). A single copper seam runs down the cleft where the layers diverge — rock split open to expose the vein. More overtly a letter-mark than concept 01, so it ties hard to “Veta,” and the strata give it a materials/geology read that suits construction. Favicon viability: strong as a solid V silhouette; the seam thins to a hairline at 16 px but the V holds. Reads more as “a brand V” than “a forecast,” which is why it places second.

03

Signal Convergence

fragments resolving into one line Runner-up

Three short, scattered teal signal ticks on the left (customs, tenders, satellite — the fragmented sources) converge into a single clean copper forecast line on the right. It tells the wedge story literally: many raw signals, fused into one number. Favicon viability: the most literal narrative but the busiest — three ticks plus a line crowd a 16 px box, so the small read is softer than 01. Best as a large/animated mark; it places third on pure favicon strength.

Recommendation: adopt 01 · Seam Forecast. It is the only mark that still says “forecast” — not merely “V” — at 16 px, it carries the tagline and the name in a single stroke, and it is materially simpler and sharper than the rejected seam-through-V. It works in one color, copper/amber on dark, and copper on light. Strata V is held as the formal letter-mark alternative; Signal Convergence as the narrative / motion variant.
